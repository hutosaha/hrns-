ABOUT US

algorythm meets human rythm

We combine the expert eye of in-industry experts (Human Rythm), with the analytics of data science (Algorithm) to ensure you get the very best candidates - passive and actively looking

As designers, developers, IT professionals and recruiters, we got fed up with how hard it is to quickly access the very best tech & digital candidates in the market.

So, two brothers and pretty goood looking Product Designer decided it was time to put it right; so we created Harness, to help Agencies, Hiring Clients and Candidates work better together; saving time, money and effort for all involved.

It's a hiring solution for the digital economy.

HOW IT WORKS

1. CLICK

Simply enter into Harness the vacancy you have, click Submit, and boom! The best tech recruiters around the world are working to find you the very best tech talent

2. SMILE

Sit back, relax and watch the best candidates come your way – no admin, no chasing, we arrange interviews and do all the hard work, and so you don’t have too!

3. HIRE

Found someone perfect for your team? Hire them! Harness allows you to focus on the things you love doing; meeting and greeting amazing people for your team!

OUR PRODUCTS

changing the game

Harness HireTM

Our premium product, Harness Hire allows you to enter details of who you need to hire, hit “Submit” and simply sit back and relax while we do the hard work. We will identify, qualify and select the best candidates from 1000’s of professional networks globally. Because we have some of the best, specialist boutique recruiters working for us, you will see those passive, and actively looking candidates – something normal hiring tools cannot do. Not sure who you need to hire, but would like to see what the very best have to offer?

Our products:

changing the game

Harness Hire

Our premium product, Harness Hire allows you to enter details of who you need to hire, hit “Submit” and simply sit back and relax while we do the hard work. We will identify, qualify and select the best candidates from 1000’s of professional networks globally. Because we have some of the best, specialist boutique recruiters working for us, you will see those passive, and actively looking candidates – something normal hiring tools cannot do. Simply click to interview, let’s us do the rest.

Harness Talent

Not sure who you need to hire, but would like to see what the very best have to offer? Harness Talent allows you to search a curated list of elite candidates – all of which are qualified, available to interview and have proven track records of delivery. Simply click to interview and only pay for those candidates you hire!

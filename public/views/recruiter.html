The Math

Did you know, the average tech recruiter generates £50 per hour in recruitment fees? Now, we’re no mathematicians, but that’s around £10,000 per month, so roughly one fee per month. So, what if we said we could improve that hourly rate to £5,000 per hour? We reckon we can, and here’s how. Around 95% of candidates that we as recruiters meet, greet, qualify, wine and dine, never actually make us any fees. So, what can you do with them? We want you to HARNESS them.

Becoming a partner of Harness, (and it’s not easy) means you will secure your agency exclusive access to two ground breaking products

​

1. Harness Hire

Live vacancies with some of the biggest, smallest and most exciting companies in the world – within a controlled environment, so you always know who you are up against, offering you more opportunities to speak to your candidates about (beats the weekend chat) and a chance at making more fees.

2. Harness Talent

You will like this. Harness Talent is a unique agency tool that could increase your productivity per head 10 fold, by making sure you get paid for every candidate you meet, greet and qualify.

You see we believe good recruiters should be paid for building relationships with the very best candidates in the market – so simply upload them into Harness, and if our clients (some of the top companies in the world) are interested, you will be informed, you arrange the interview, and you take the fee! Oh, and it only takes 23 seconds to upload them.
